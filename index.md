## PDF Files and Documents

PDF Reader - Smart Toolkit processes your PDF files, images and documents **locally on your device**.

Your files remain under your control.

We do **not** upload, store or transmit your PDF files or documents to our own servers.

Files are only shared if you explicitly choose to use your device's sharing features.
